Baseball Pitching Types

There are 3 different types of Pitches: Fastball, Breaking balls, and Change ups(off-speed pitch).

  • Fastball:

    • Four-seam Fastball
    • Two-seam Fastball
    • Cutter
    • Sinker
    • Split-finger Ball

    Breaking Balls:

    • Curveball
    • Slider
    • Slurve
    • 12-6 Curveball
    • Knuckle Curve
    • Screwball

    Chnage-ups(Off-speed pitch):

    • Change up
    • Circle Change up
    • Forkball
    • Knuckle Ball
